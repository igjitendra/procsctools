Register.csc.gov.in se lekar monthly Rs 40,000+ income tak - complete roadmap.

CSC / Digital Seva Kendra ek government-authorized center jo 30+ digital services deta hai. Self-employment ka best model — Rs 25,000-40,000 monthly income. Complete guide.

Eligibility

  • 18+ age
  • 10+2 minimum
  • Basic computer
  • TEC certificate (online Rs 1,479)

Registration Process

  1. TEC exam: cscentrepreneur.in — Rs 1,479
  2. Apply: register.csc.gov.in — TEC, Aadhaar, PAN, bank, photo
  3. DM approval: 2-4 hafte
  4. CSC ID: Digital Seva portal login
