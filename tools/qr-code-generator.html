Frequently Asked Questions

Is this QR Code Generator Pro completely free? ▼

Can I generate UPI Payment QR codes for my shop? ▼
Yes! Enter your UPI VPA ID (e.g. 9876543210@paytm), shop payee name, and optional amount to create instant payment QRs for PhonePe, GPay, and Paytm.

No, these are static QR codes that will work permanently without any expiration date.
What is the difference between PNG and SVG export? ▼
PNG is ideal for digital sharing and social media, while SVG is a vector format perfect for high-quality printing on banners, posters, and business cards.
Are my details uploaded to any server? ▼
No! All QR codes are rendered locally inside your web browser. Your private data is never saved or uploaded.
