🚀 How to Use & Guidelines
  1. 1. Login: Access AI Studio with your Google account. Connect Google Drive if prompted for storage.
  2. 2. Select Type: Click a photo category card above — the prompt will auto-copy to clipboard.
  3. 3. Paste: In AI Studio, Paste (Ctrl+V) the prompt into the chat box.
  4. 4. Settings: On the right sidebar, select Aspect Ratio: 4:5.
  5. 5. Upload: Click the '+' or Upload icon below the box to add your photo.
  6. 6. Generate: Click the Run button. Wait 15 seconds for your HD passport photo!
