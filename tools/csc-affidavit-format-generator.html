Frequently Asked Questions

What is an affidavit and when is it needed? ▼
An affidavit is a written sworn statement of fact made under oath before a Notary Public or Oath Commissioner. It is commonly required for Name Change, Address Proof, DOB Correction, Marriage Certificate, School admission, Markshet correction, and government portal submissions.
Is this CSC Affidavit Format Generator free for VLEs? ▼
Yes, 100% free with unlimited downloads. CSC VLEs, Jan Seva Kendra operators, and individuals can generate and print affidavits without any fees or registration.
Which stamp paper value is required for an affidavit? ▼
Non-judicial e-stamp paper of ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, or ₹100 is typically used depending on your state's Stamp Duty Act and the specific purpose of the affidavit.
How to print the format directly onto physical stamp paper? ▼
Adjust the 'Stamp Top Margin Space' slider in the editor to leave the top portion blank (matching your physical e-stamp print area). Then feed your stamp paper into the printer and click 'Print Now'.
Is the generated format legally valid for Notary attestation? ▼
Yes, the legal phrasing and verification clauses strictly follow standard Indian legal drafting templates. Once printed on non-judicial stamp paper and signed before a registered Notary Public, it becomes legally binding.
