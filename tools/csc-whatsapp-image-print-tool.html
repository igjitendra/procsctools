Frequently Asked Questions

Is my WhatsApp document data safe on Pro CSC Tools? ▼
Yes, 100% safe. All crop, rotate, and PDF/JPG processing happens locally in your web browser using HTML5 Canvas & OpenCV. No images are uploaded to any server.
Which documents can I print on A4 paper with this tool? ▼
You can print Aadhaar Cards, PAN Cards, Voter IDs, Driving Licenses, Passports, Bank Passbooks, Land Records, Markshet copies, and any photo received on WhatsApp.
How to clear dark or blurry WhatsApp photos before printing? ▼
Select 'Magic' or 'Clear Text' enhancement in Step 2. Use the Brightness, Contrast, and Sharpness sliders to turn dark phone photos into clean scanner-like output.
Can I print front and back sides of an ID card on a single A4 sheet? ▼
Yes! Choose ID Card mode, upload Front and Back photos, and the tool will align both sides on a single A4 page vertically or horizontally with standard card dimensions.
Is this tool free for CSC VLEs and Jan Seva Kendra owners? ▼
Yes! It is 100% free with unlimited scans, PDF downloads, and direct printing. No registration or payment is required.
