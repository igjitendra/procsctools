Why Add Name and Date on Passport Photos?

When applying for competitive government exams in India such as SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, Railway RRB, IBPS Banking, UP Police, Bihar Police, MPPSC, and NTA exams, candidate photos are strictly scrutinized. Most recruitment notifications state that candidate's full name and date of photo capture must be printed clearly on the bottom of the passport photograph.

With our free Photo Name Joiner online tool, CSC VLEs, Cyber Cafe operators, and students can create perfectly aligned photo cards with custom text color, border styles, and text positioning within seconds. No complex Photoshop skills required!

Major Indian recruitment boards (SSC, Railway, Banking, Police) require recent photographs with candidate name and date printed on them to prevent impersonation and verify application validity.
