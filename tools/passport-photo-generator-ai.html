How to Use Passport Photo Generator AI

Follow these 5 simple steps to get your professional passport photo in 15 seconds.

1

Select Category

Click on any photo category card above (Male, Female, Hijab, Turban, Student, Baby). The prompt auto-copies instantly.

2

Open ChatGPT

ChatGPT will automatically open in a new tab. Log in using your free Google or email account.

3

Upload Your Photo

Click the 📎 paperclip icon below the message box and attach your clear front-facing portrait photo.

Send & Download

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Passport Photo Generator AI completely free? ▼
Yes! This tool and all Pro CSC Tools are 100% free with no registration or hidden fees.
Will the AI generated photo meet Indian official passport standards? ▼
Our smart prompts specify exact official parameters: 35mm x 45mm ratio, pure white background, centered face, even lighting, and visible ears. Verify with a professional studio for final printing.
How to paste prompt in ChatGPT on mobile? ▼
Clicking any card copies the prompt to your clipboard. In ChatGPT, long press inside the chat box and select 'Paste', then attach your photo using the paperclip icon.
What type of selfie or photo should I upload? ▼
Upload a clear, front-facing portrait photo taken in bright natural light where your full face is visible without shadows or sunglasses.

Yes! We provide tailored prompt presets for School Students, College Admissions, Hijab/Turban religious headwear, Infant babies, and Senior Citizens.
