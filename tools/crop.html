What is an Image Cropping Tool?

An image cropping tool lets you cut out a specific portion of a photograph or picture to focus on what matters most. Whether you need a square profile photo for WhatsApp DP, a 16:9 thumbnail for YouTube, or a passport-size photo for a government form, this free online photo cropper handles it all — right in your browser, with no software to install.

Our image crop tool online free uses the powerful Cropper.js library to give you professional-grade cropping controls: drag-to-resize crop box, aspect ratio lock, rotate, flip, zoom and custom pixel dimensions. Everything runs locally on your device, so your images stay 100% private and secure.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Use 1:1 ratio for WhatsApp DP, Instagram posts and Aadhaar photos
  • Use 3:4 ratio for passport-size photos and Indian government forms
  • Use 16:9 ratio for YouTube thumbnails and presentation slides
  • Use 9:16 ratio for Instagram Stories, Reels and WhatsApp Status
  • Set custom pixel dimensions for exact size requirements like 200×200 or 600×600
  • Use rotate before cropping if your photo is sideways from a phone camera
