Frequently Asked Questions

How to resize signature image size to 10kb to 20kb? ▼
Upload your signature photo, select unit (Pixels or CM), enter desired width/height, check 'Compress Image To Specific Size', enter 18 KB, and click 'Resize Signature Image'.
What is the standard signature size in cm for online forms? ▼
Standard signature dimensions for government portals (SSC, UPSC, IBPS) are 3.5 cm x 1.5 cm (140x60 px) with file size between 10KB and 20KB.
Is this signature resizer tool safe and private? ▼
Yes, 100% safe! All image processing happens locally in your browser canvas. Your signature image is never uploaded to any server.
