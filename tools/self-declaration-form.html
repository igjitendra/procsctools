Self Declaration Form (स्वघोषणा पत्र) kya hai?

Self Declaration Form, jise Hindi me Swaghoshna Patra ya स्वप्रमाणित घोषणा-पत्र kehte hain, ek aisa document hai jisme aap khud apni personal details ko sach maankar declare karte ho. Aajkal bahut se kaamo me affidavit (stamp paper) ki jagah simple self declaration accept kiya jaata hai, jisse aapka time aur paisa dono bachta hai.

Ye free online tool aapko Hindi aur English dono me ready-to-print self declaration banane deta hai — bas details bharo, signature lagao aur PDF download karo. Koi login nahi, koi charge nahi, aur aapka data server par upload nahi hota.

Kahan-kahan kaam aata hai?

  • School / college admission aur scholarship forms
  • Government yojana (PM Awas, ration card, pension) me income/residence declaration
  • Bank, KYC aur job application me self declaration
  • CSC / Jan Seva Kendra par citizens ke liye instant declaration print
  • Address, age ya marital status declare karne ke liye

Self Declaration Form kaise banaye? (Step by step)

  • Step 1: Naam, pita/pati ka naam, umar, vyavsay aur pura pata bharo.
  • Step 2: Sthan (place) aur dinank (date) select karo.
  • Step 3: Signature mouse/finger se banao ya image upload karo. Chaho to passport photo bhi lagao.
  • Step 4: “Download PDF” dabao → Destination me “Save as PDF” choose karo. Bas ho gaya!
