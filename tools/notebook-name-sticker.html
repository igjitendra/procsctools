Frequently Asked Questions

How many name stickers fit on one A4 paper? ▼
Our tool automatically arranges 10 name stickers in a 2x5 grid on a single A4 page without any overflow or page cuts.
How to change label language to Hindi? ▼
Click the "Switch to Hindi Labels" button in the settings panel to convert Class, Section, Roll, and Subject into Hindi (कक्षा, वर्ग, रोल, विषय).
Is my uploaded student photo safe? ▼
Yes, 100% safe! All images are processed locally in your browser canvas. No student data or photos are uploaded to any server.
