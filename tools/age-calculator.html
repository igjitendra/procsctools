What Is an Exact Age Calculator?

An age calculator online free tool finds the time completed between your date of birth and today. Instead of estimating only the year, this calculator breaks the result into completed years, remaining months and days. It also converts your age into total months, days, hours, minutes and seconds for a more detailed view.

Manual age calculation can become confusing because months have different lengths and leap years add an extra day. This date of birth calculator handles those calendar differences automatically. Enter the same birth date shown on your Aadhaar card, birth certificate, marksheet or another trusted document to get a useful result in seconds.
