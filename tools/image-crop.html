Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Image Cropping tool completely free? ▼
Yes, it is 100% free with no hidden fees, no sign-up requirement, and no watermarks on your cropped photos.
Are my images saved on your server? ▼
No! All cropping operations run strictly in your local web browser. Your images are private and never uploaded to any server.
Which aspect ratios and dimensions are supported? ▼
We support standard ratios such as 1:1 (passport photos), 4:3, 16:9 (youtube/banners), 9:16 (vertical story), 3:4, 3:2, 21:9, and custom manual pixel inputs.
What file formats can I crop? ▼
You can upload JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, and BMP image files up to 10MB in size.
Can I rotate or flip my image before cropping? ▼
Yes! Built-in controls allow 90-degree left/right rotation and horizontal or vertical flipping before saving your final crop.
