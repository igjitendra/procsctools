Aadhar Card PVC Print Karte Waqt Ye 5 Galtiyan Na Karein

90% CSC operators ye galtiyan karte hain — aap kaise bachein.

Har VLE ne shuruaat me ye galtiyan ki hain — blur print, cut text, wrong colors. 5 most common galtiyan aur exact solutions.

Galti 1: Adobe/Chrome Se Direct Print

Problem: Scaling galat — PVC size wrong.
Solution: Hamesha Windows Photos se print karein.

Galti 2: Matte Paper

Problem: Colors dull, PVC ke baad aur bekar.
Solution: 200 GSM glossy paper (Rs 2-3/sheet).

Galti 3: PDF Password Format Galat

Problem: "Password galat" error.
Solution: Naam ke pehle 4 CAPITAL + YYYY. Example: RAHU1998. Detail.

Galti 4: Manual Front-Back Crop

Problem: Time waste, alignment galat.
Solution: Aadhar Crop Tool — auto detect + alignment, 10 sec.

Galti 5: Laminator Temp Galat

Problem: Wrinkle ya loose lamination.
Solution: 130-140°C, 4-6 sec pass.

Pro Tip: Ye 5 fix — quality 90% up, customer complaints khatam.

Frequently Asked Questions

Windows Photos hi kyu?

Glossy vs matte kitna difference?

Glossy par colors 40-50% zyada vibrant.

Laminator budget me?

Skywalker A4 / Meba — Rs 1500-2500.

Blur — printer galat?

Nahi, source DPI kam. Tool + Windows Photos best quality.

Blank card vs pouch?

Blank card durable, pouch sasta — chhote setup ke liye better.
