4x6 Size Kya Hota Hai?

4x6 inch (10.16 x 15.24 cm) standard photo paper size hai. Ek 4x6 sheet me Aadhaar ka front + back dono fit ho jaate hain, jise PVC pouch me laminate karke professional card banta hai.
